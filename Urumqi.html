Turkestan, heart of Asia

East Turkestanalso known as Uyghuristan, is a loosely-defined geographical and historical region in Central Asia, which varies in meaning by context and usage. The medieval Persian toponym "Turkestan" and its derivatives were not, however, used by the local population. The Uyghur name for the Tarim Basin is Altishahr, which means "Six Cities" in Uyghur. China from the Han Dynasty to Tang Dynasty had called an overlapping area the "Western Regions". The parts of this area controlled by China were termed "Xinjiang" starting in the 18th century.
